Another stop on the journey through the tidyverse and this time it’s one of the most interesting and mysterious corners (I think) of the entire suite of tidyverse packages - tidy evaluation. My goal for the talk was to introduce ideas behind tidy evaluation in an interactive manner. To do that, I’ve used mostly exactly the same approach as Lionel Henry (main contributor to rlang that hosts tidy evaluation) outlined in this WIP book creatively called “Tidy evaluation”.
